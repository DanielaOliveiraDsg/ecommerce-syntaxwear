## SECTION STRUCTURE

The site is organized into the following sections for a clear and intuitive user experience:

- **Header**: Includes the logo, main navigation with product categories (Men's, Women's, Outlet), and quick access links to our stores, company information, user account, help, and shopping cart.

- **Hero**: Main banner that highlights a featured product, which in this case is the "Krypton One", with call-to-action buttons for "View models" and "Buy" to immediately capture the user's interest.

- **Product Categories**: Visual section with quick access to the main shoe categories: Casual, Sports, Modern, and Futuristic.

- **Featured Products**: A grid that highlights specific models and collections, such as the "Krypton One", with direct links to the men's and women's sections.

- **Footer**: Contains a form to subscribe to the newsletter, links to social media (Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok, Facebook), and detailed navigation through the product categories, company information, and physical and online stores.
